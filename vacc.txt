The Moderna COVID-19 vaccine, codenamed mRNA-1273 and sold under the brand name Spikevax, is a COVID-19 vaccine developed by American company Modernai.

The Oxford, AstraZeneca vaccine is a vaccine that aims to protect against COVID-19.

Pfizer-BioNTech (COMIRNATY) vaccine was 95% effective at preventing laboratory-confirmed infection with the virus that causes COVID-19.
